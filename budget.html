Managing Your Student Budget

Students with accommodation usually receive around P1500 allowance. Managing this money properly is important to survive the entire month.

Food

Most students spend about P850 on food during the month. This includes simple foods like noodles, bread, snacks, and the supper meal provided at school.

Transport

Transport is usually not a big expense because BAC provides free transport for students. Students mainly use taxis or combis occasionally when going to town or visiting friends.

Internet & Airtime

Students receive BTC SIM cards with around 6GB of data per day. This helps with studying, watching tutorials, and completing assignments online.
