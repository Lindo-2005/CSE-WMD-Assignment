Welcome to Campus Survival Hub

A student-built platform designed to help hostel residents survive campus life, manage their allowance, and access essential student resources. While focused on Botswana School of Business Sciences, the platform applies to student living across multiple campuses.

- BAC Student Guide

Our platform provides practical solutions for student accommodation, budgeting, and daily survival in campus hostels.
