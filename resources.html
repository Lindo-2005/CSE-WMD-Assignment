Helpful Student Resources

BAC students have access to several resources that help with studying, assignments, and daily student life.

Study Resources

Students can use the library, online tutorials, and learning platforms to improve their understanding of course material.

Computer Labs

BAC provides computer labs where students can complete assignments, especially those who do not have laptops.

Internet Access

Students have access to campus WiFi and BTC SIM cards with around 6GB of data daily, helping them access online learning materials.
