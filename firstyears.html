What You Are Allowed to Bring

BAC dormitories such as Main Campus, Windsor Inn, Block 7, BNPC, and Nicopolis have strict rules about what students are allowed to bring. Students are allowed to bring essential appliances like a bar fridge, kettle, and iron.

Other appliances such as stoves or sandwich makers are not allowed because the college considers them safety risks. Students are encouraged to bring personal essentials such as toiletries and cleaning supplies.

Lack of Information About Dorms

Many first-year students arrive without knowing what the dormitories look like, especially off-campus residences like Windsor Inn, Nicopolis, and Block 7. There are very few photos or guides available online, so students often arrive without knowing what to expect.

Common First-Year Mistakes

One of the most common mistakes first-year students make is spending their allowance too quickly. Because students are excited about their new freedom, they sometimes spend money on unnecessary things early in the month.

This can be a problem because first-year students often receive their allowance later than returning students while waiting for their FNB cards.

Adjusting to Independent Living

Adjusting to independent living can be difficult for many students. Managing food, money, and responsibilities without parental support requires time and discipline.

Learning to budget, buy groceries, and manage time effectively is an important part of surviving the first year at college.
